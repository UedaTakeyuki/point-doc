Privacy Policy

Welcome on CONNECT!

The purpose of this document is to clarify how we respect your privacy and use your personal information as necessary to operate our products and services.

1. login account

The standard login system of Atelier Ueda, which Connect also uses, is implemented using the service Firebase Auth provided by Google for blocking illegal use and password stealing. Inside our service, we don't use your login account and password directly. Instead, we use the random string provided by Firebase as your credential information. Due to the separation of login service from our service, There is no possibility that our programmers will look at your login account or password unintentionally.

Although we don't use your login account and password directly, we can still get your login account information from the API provided by Firebase. When we need to contact you, we will get your information in the manner described above and contact you.

CONNECT’s main activity is to provide a remote CONNECTing service which show the latest picture and data from your device on website https://CONNECT.uedasoft.com, https://CONNECT3.uedasoft.com, and to some accessory sub-domains such as https://atelierueda.uedasoft.com, or through its mobile application (hereafter ‘Services’).

CONNECT’s policy for respecting the privacy of its users elevates to a very high requirements level. Our commitments for protecting your personal data can be read here. In general, CONNECT does not collect personal data from the typical use of its Services.

Processing of information requests as well as the registration of certain Services may however require that users communicate some personal data such as their name, first name, contact info and e-mail address. This data is collected and processed by CONNECT for the following purposes:

  • - Technical management of the account(s) of the user and of the available Services;
  • - Management of information requests and job applications;
  • - Management of delisting requests and requests associated with the right to access, rectify and delete personal data;
  • - The security of CONNECT’s information system;
  • - Respecting our legal and regulatory obligations.
  • - By sending an electronic mail to ueda@laterierdueda.com.
